> **Goal:** Turn every email into a reason for LPs to open the app, and every app session into proof that GYC makes their investing life insanely easy.

## 2. Seinfeld Daily Emails

Short, personality-driven emails. One story, one point, one CTA. Written like you talk.

### Rotating Theme Bank (pull 1 per day):

**"Things I learned the hard way"**
- The first deal I almost invested in that turned out to be garbage
- Why I stopped trusting projected IRRs at face value
- The GP who ghosted investors after closing
- How I lost sleep over a deal and what I do differently now

**"Behind the scenes"**
- Feature I'm building this week and why
- A conversation I had with a GP that changed my mind
- What I look at first when a new deal hits my desk
- How I decide which deals make the cut for the marketplace

**"Investor psychology"**
- Why most LPs never get past their first deal
- The analysis paralysis trap (and how the Buy Box fixes it)
- "I'll wait for a better deal" — why that's usually fear talking
- The difference between being cautious and being stuck

**"Quick wins"**
- One thing to check on every PPM before you sign
- The question I always ask GPs on a webinar
- How to read a waterfall structure in 60 seconds
- What your K-1 is actually telling you

**Format:**
```
Subject: [Story hook — no deal names, no clickbait]

Hey {first_name},

[2-3 paragraph story]

[One takeaway]

[Soft CTA to app or reply]

— Pascal

P.S. [Teaser for tomorrow or a feature plug]
```

- Moderation: Pascal approves before publish

## 8. First 10 GP & LP Onboarding Plan

### GP Onboarding (First 10 Operators)

**Selection criteria:**
- Pascal has personal relationship
- Track record of 3+ completed deals
- Willing to participate in office hours / webinars
- Has InvestClearly profile (preferred)

**Onboarding sequence per GP:**
1. **Personal call** — Walk through what GYC offers (LP network stats, analytics dashboard, investor insights)
2. **Guided onboarding** — Do the 7-step GP onboarding wizard together on a screen share
3. **First deal listing** — Help them upload their first deal with deck, metrics, documents
4. **Profile buildout** — Complete sponsor profile with headshots, track record, InvestClearly link
5. **Podcast appearance** — Schedule them on the podcast (content + promotion)
6. **Office hours intro** — Bring them to a live office hours for Q&A with LPs
7. **Analytics walkthrough** — Show them the GP dashboard after 2 weeks of LP activity
8. **Feedback loop** — Monthly check-in on what's working, what they need

**Email to GPs after onboarding:**
```
Subject: Your first 2 weeks on GYC Dealflow

Hey [GP Name],

Here's what happened since you listed [Deal Name]:

- [X] LPs viewed your deal
- [X] saved it to their pipeline
- [X] started DD
- [X] investors are in the Academy (serious capital)

Our LP network: [X] accredited investors, $[X]M in aggregate investable capital,
top asset class preferences: [list]

Next steps:
- Join office hours [date] to meet investors live
- Your podcast episode drops [date]

— Pascal
```

### LP Onboarding (First 10 Investors)

**Selection criteria:**
- Accredited investors
- Mix of new (first syndication) and experienced (5+ deals)
- Willing to give feedback and leave reviews
- Ideally different goal profiles (Cash Flow, Tax Reduction, Equity Growth)

**Onboarding sequence per LP:**
1. **Personal welcome call** — Understand their situation, goals, experience
2. **Buy box setup** — Walk through the 21-question wizard together
3. **Marketplace tour** — Show filtered results, match scores, deal detail pages
4. **DD walkthrough** — Pick a live deal and walk through the DD checklist together
5. **Portfolio import** — Help them log existing investments
6. **Income roadmap** — Build their personalized plan
7. **Office hours invite** — Get them on the next live call
8. **Community intro** — Introduce them to other LPs and relevant GPs
9. **Weekly check-in** (first month) — "What's confusing? What's missing? What would make you tell a friend?"
10. **Review ask** — After 30 days, ask for testimonial + app store review

## 10. Email Design Principles

1. **Plain text feel, rich content** — Emails should read like Pascal writing to a friend, not a marketing blast
2. **One CTA per email** — Every email drives to one action
3. **Screenshots are the hero** — The before/after format makes the value undeniable
4. **Mobile-first** — Most investors check email on their phone
5. **Reply-friendly** — "Just reply to this email" in every sign-off builds trust and generates conversations
6. **Consistent sign-off** — Always "— Pascal" with a P.S.
7. **Segmentation:**
   - Free tier: Feature promotion emails tease Academy-gated features
   - Academy: Full access content, DD deep dives, Pascal's picks with scorecard
   - GP list: Separate cadence with LP network stats, platform updates
8. **Unsubscribe respect** — Always honor, never guilt. Clean list > big list.
