2. Authorization to Display & Distribute Deal Materials

You grant GYC a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to:

  • Display your deal information, offering summary, and key terms on the Platform
  • Share your pitch deck, PPM, and other uploaded documents with registered investors on the Platform
  • Include your deal in filtered search results, deal alerts, and personalized recommendations sent to investors whose investment criteria match your offering
  • Feature your deal in email communications, newsletters, and marketing materials distributed to the GYC investor network
  • Facilitate direct introductions between interested investors and your designated Investor Relations contact

This license continues for as long as your deal is active on the Platform. You may request removal of your deal at any time by contacting us.

3. Presentation Recording & Distribution

If you participate in a GYC live deal presentation event, you consent to:

  • The session being recorded in its entirety (audio, video, and screen share)
  • The recording being shared with investors in the GYC database, including those who did not attend the live event
  • The recording being embedded on your deal page within the Platform
  • GYC using excerpts from the recording in marketing materials to promote future events

4. Data Accuracy & Representations

You represent and warrant that:

  • All deal information, financial projections, terms, and materials you provide are truthful, accurate, and not misleading

  • Your offering is properly structured in compliance with applicable securities laws, including but not limited to Regulation D of the Securities Act of 1933
  • You will promptly notify GYC if any material facts about your offering change after submission
  • You will update your deal information on the Platform if terms, status, or availability change

5. Regulatory Compliance

For 506(c) offerings: You acknowledge that GYC may share your deal materials with accredited investors and that GYC will take reasonable steps to verify accreditation status before facilitating introductions.

For 506(b) offerings: You acknowledge that your deal will not be publicly marketed or displayed as a deal card on the Platform. Your deal will be stored in our database for portfolio tracking purposes only and will only be visible to investors with whom a pre-existing substantive relationship exists.

GYC is not a broker-dealer and does not provide investment advice, make investment recommendations, or facilitate securities transactions. This Agreement remains in effect for as long as you have an active operator account on the Platform. Either party may terminate by providing written notice. Upon termination, GYC will remove your active deal listings within a reasonable timeframe, but may retain records of your account and agreement acceptance for legal and compliance purposes.
